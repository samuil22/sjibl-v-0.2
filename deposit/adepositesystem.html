    AL WADIAH CURRENT DEPOSIT

    Al Wadiah Current Deposit (AWCD) is essentially an Al-Wadiah Yad-Dhamanah (Guarantee) contract between the depositors and the Bank, which allows withdrawal of depositors’ money on demand. This account can be transected as many times as required. While the depositors permits the Bank to use their money according to Islamic Shariah. No proﬁt is disbursed in these accounts and depositors do not bear any loss.

    CORE FEATURES

    No Profit Applicable

    REQUIREMENTS AND ACCOUNT DETAILS

    AWCA is designed for those individuals and institutions, who want to operate their ﬁnancial activities as per Islamic Shariah

  • Any Bangladeshi citizen, singly or jointly with other adults, having age 18 years or above with sound mind, and is not declared insolvent by any Court of Law of the Land
  • Any proprietorship/partnership ﬁrm, limited company (private/public), educational institution, club, association, socio-economic organization can also open this account. as per policy of the Bank submitting required documents
  • AWCA follows the Principle of Islami Shariah or Interest-free transactions
  • Account can be opened in Single or Joint names
  • Minimum initial Balance is BDT 5,000
  • No proﬁt will be applicable
  • Unlimited number of deposits or withdrawals facility
  • MICR Cheque Book facility
  • Any branch Banking facility
  • Monthly statement facility
  • Bank accepts and supports Standing Instructions
  • Facilities of using Alternative Banking Channels through Debit Card, SMS and Internet Banking
  • Free Cash withdrawal from all ATMs of the Bank
  • Minors are discouraged to open this account
  • Maintenance fees will be realized on a half yearly basis
  • Source Tax and Excise Duty to be deducted as per the law of the land
  • Account opening form duly ﬁlled up and signed by account holder(s)
  • 02 Copies passport size recent photographs of the applicant(s) duly attested by the introducer
  • 01 copy passport size recent photograph of the Nominee duly attested by the account holder
  • NID/Passport/Any other valid photo identity of applicant(s) and nominee
  • Updated TIN Certiﬁcate of applicant ( if any )
  • Signature & details of Introducer
  • Proprietorship Firm – Valid Trade License
  • Partnership Firm – Partnership Deed duly signed by the partners, Partnership Account Agreement
  • Limited Company – Certiﬁed copies of ‘Memorandum & Articles of Association’, Certiﬁcate of Incorporation, Resolution of the Board of Directors for opening and operation of accounts, List of Directors with their details
  • Club/Society/School/College – Certiﬁed copies of ‘Memorandum & Articles of Association’, Bye Laws & Regulations/Constitution, Resolution for opening and operation of account, Updated List of Directors/ Ofﬁce Bearers/Governing Body/Managing Committee
  • Trust – Certiﬁed copies of Deed of Trust, Resolution of the Board of Trustees for opening and operation of account, Updated List of Members of the Trustee Board
